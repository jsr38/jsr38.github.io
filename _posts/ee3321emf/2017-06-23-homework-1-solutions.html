\(\mathbf{A} = 2\mathbf{i} - 2\mathbf{j} + \mathbf{k}\) and \(\mathbf{B} = 2\mathbf{i} - \mathbf{j} + 3\mathbf{k}\) and \(\mathbf{C} = 4\mathbf{i} + 2\mathbf{j} - 2 \mathbf{k}\).

\begin{equation} \mathbf{A}\cdot\mathbf{C} = 8 - 4 - 2 = 2 \end{equation} \begin{equation} \mathbf{B}\cdot\mathbf{C} = 8 - 2 - 6 = 0 \end{equation}

\(\mathbf{C}\) is not perpendicular to both \(\mathbf{A}\) and \(\mathbf{B}\)!
